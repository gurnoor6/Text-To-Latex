Our Idea

Hello Visitor.There have been multiple instances in our life where we have scanned our handwritten mathematical documents and find ourselves frustrated, not being able to use it for searching or copying the text. Also, it would be an insurmountable task to go through the entire document, considering the huge size of it. Our project aims to eliminate the extra effort put in by converting the handwritten text to LaTeX script, and hence easing the difficulty faced and also providing the scope for formatting and designing better.
